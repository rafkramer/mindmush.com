When you use our mobile games and services, we may collect the following types of information:

We collect non-personal information, including:

  • Device information (e.g., type, operating system, IP address).

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our games and services.
  • Personalize in-game content and advertisements.
  • Improve game performance and user experience.
  • Process in-app purchases through app stores.
  • Offer rewards through Tapjoy's offerwalls.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share non-personal information with third-party service providers like AppLovin, Tapjoy, and app stores to facilitate ads, game performance analytics, and in-app purchases.

Specifically for the "FaceKit" app:

  • Face Collection Data: The app collects face-related data using Apple's TrueDepth API and ARKit when the user explicitly initiates a facial scan. This data includes 3D facial geometry (face mesh), depth data used to construct the mesh, a texture image mapped to the mesh, and derived facial measurements calculated from the mesh. No face data is collected passively or without user action.
  • Use of Face Data: Face data is used solely for facial proportion analysis and aesthetic evaluation. The app calculates facial measurements such as symmetry, proportions, and feature ratios, generates a beauty score and visual analysis, and enables before-and-after comparisons over time. Face data is not used for identity verification, authentication, Face ID, biometric identification, or user recognition.
  • Face Data Storage and Retention: Facial mesh and texture data are stored securely in cloud storage provided by Supabase for the purpose of performing analysis and allowing users to access their results. Face data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide these features and is deleted when the user deletes a scan or their account.
  • Third-Party Sharing of Face Data: The app does not share face data with any third parties. Face data is not sold, licensed, or disclosed to external entities.
  • User Consent and Control: All facial scans are initiated explicitly by the user. Users have full control over their face data and may delete their scans or account at any time, which permanently removes associated face data from our systems.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect the information we collect. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
